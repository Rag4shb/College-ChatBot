Our Society & Institution

Introduction of the Society

The Chikodi Liberal Education Society has come up to this present state of glory and magnitude as a result of its immense stress on the education of the surrounding rural masses. The society was founded by the sincere efforts of the patrons of the locality in 1962. Ever since it has been graced as the premier society of Chikodi and its surrounding region.

Today the society through a single campus is providing education from pre-primary to graduation along with Teacher Training courses. At present, the society has thirteen educational institutions with more than 8,500 students studying on the campus annually.

The glorious institution C.L.E. Society's Shri B. C. Gangal College of Education (B.Ed.), Chikodi has been a source of inspiration for a bright future of students and has maintained reputation, quality, and academic standards.

The college is recognized by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), Government of Karnataka and was affiliated with Karnatak University, Dharwad from the academic year 2004-05. From the academic year 2010-11 onwards, the institution is affiliated with Rani Channamma University, Belagavi.
