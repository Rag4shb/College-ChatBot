About the College

A centre of excellence for BCA education in North Karnataka.

C.L.E. Society's BCA College, Chikodi is committed to academic excellence and professional training in Computer Applications. The college provides modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and industry-focused education to prepare students for successful IT careers.

Our Degree

Bachelor of Computer Applications

A comprehensive 3-year undergraduate program designed for the IT industry.

The BCA program at C.L.E. Society's BCA College is a rigorous 3-year undergraduate course covering Programming, Database Management, Web Development, Networking, Python, Artificial Intelligence, and Project Development. Students gain real-world skills through labs, workshops, and industry projects.

State-of-the-art Labs

Modern software & hardware infrastructure for hands-on learning.

Expert Faculty

Industry-experienced educators with strong academic credentials.

Emerging Tech

AI, Python, Cloud Computing, and Networking in the curriculum.

Career Support

Internships, placements, and career development guidance.
